# Intersand Company

## Company Overview

**Intersand** is a Canadian cat litter manufacturer and one of the world's largest producers of premium cat litter. The company has been in operation for over 30 years, committed to innovation, pet well-being, and eco-responsible solutions.

**Founded:** 1992
**Headquarters:** Varennes, Quebec, Canada
**US Headquarters:** Windsor, Colorado, USA
**Global Reach:** Exports to over 60 countries

## Company History

### Origins

- Founded in 1992 by **Stéphane Chevigny** in Varennes, Quebec
- Started while Chevigny was working for the family business Provalcid
- The founding inspiration came from Chevigny asking himself: "How can I make this task more pleasant?" when cleaning his cat's litter
- Initial operations took place in the old family barn with a single production line and one silo
- The company's mission became creating "the world's best cat litter"

### Growth Timeline

- **Early years:** Operations in family barn in Varennes
- **Expansion:** Moved to Boucherville (located near railway infrastructure), enabling significant growth
- **2004:** Entered the Asian market after establishing presence in Canada and Europe
- **2017:** Became a zero-waste company
- **2021:** Began conducting annual greenhouse gas (GHG) assessments
- **Present:** Operates 5 state-of-the-art manufacturing facilities across North America

## Manufacturing & Operations

### OdourLock Technology (Flagship Product)

#### Patented Innovation

- **Smart Odor Shield Technology** - Blocks ammonia release
- Neutralizes urine and feces odors for **40 days**
- Won **Editors' Stand Out Product Award** (2014) from Pet Product News magazine

#### How It Works

- Cat urine doesn't naturally contain ammonia
- Ammonia forms when urine contacts oxygen and bacteria in the litter box
- OdourLock technology activates upon contact with urine to prevent ammonia formation
- Provides odor control for 40+ days

#### Performance Features

- Made from 100% pure natural sodium bentonite clay from Wyoming mines
- Absorbs up to **6x its weight** in liquid
- Lasts **40% longer** than conventional litter
- Users consume **45% less litter per year** compared to competing clay litters
- **99.9% dust-free**
- Minimal tracking on floors and furniture
- Respiratory-safe formulation

#### Specialized Products

- **OdourLock MaxCare** - Health monitoring cat litter

### ECOLIFE (Eco-Friendly Line)

#### Sustainability Features
